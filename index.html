Process

Clarity from first measure to final clean.

A disciplined workflow that removes surprises, aligns on aesthetics, and keeps the site tidy every day.

Step 01

Consult & measure

On-site review of substrates, moisture, and tolerances. We map layouts, cuts, and transitions before a single tile is set.

Step 02

Design alignment

We collaborate with your designer to finalize grout lines, trim profiles, and edge details that honor the original vision.

Step 03

Precision installation

Laser-guided layout, zero-lippage setting, and clean joints. Waterproofing, flood tests, and heated floor integration on request.

Step 04

Quality handoff

Detailed cleanup, curing guidance, and a final walkthrough with punch-list resolution before we call it complete.
