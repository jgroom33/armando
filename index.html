Process

Builder-grade organization, showroom-grade finish.

A clean, predictable path from spec to sign-off keeps designers confident and clients calm.

01

Spec + site study

Review drawings, substrates, moisture, and tolerances. We map transitions, grout widths, and edge details upfront.

02

Protection + prep

Surface protection, leveling, waterproofing, and heat mats as needed. We stage materials and confirm cuts before setting.

03

Install + QC

Laser layout, aligned joints, and precise miters. Daily cleanup and photo updates to keep the team aligned.

04

Handoff + care

Final polish, cure guidance, and punch-list resolution. We stand behind the work with a clear warranty.
